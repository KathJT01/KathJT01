**Client Onboarding Process**
1. **Initial Contact**  
   - Send introductory email with company overview and services.
2. **Discovery Call**  
   - Schedule a call to discuss the client’s needs and expectations.
3. **Proposal Submission**  
   - Draft and send a proposal based on the call’s outcomes.
4. **Contract Signing**  
   - Once the client agrees, send contract for signature.
5. **Project Kickoff**  
   - Begin work on agreed tasks, including timeline management and regular check-ins.
